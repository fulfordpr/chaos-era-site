Welcome to the CHAOS ERA

CHAOS ERA is a moden metalcore band formed in the midwest. The band has built a reputation for punishing live performances and a sound that fuses crushing breakdowns with catchy choruses. High production value and professionalism are at the core of their ethos, setting them apart in a genre often defined by its rawness.

Drawing influence from the all corners of modern metal, CHAOS ERA delivers your soundtrack to the end of the world. A mixtape of aggressive guitars, dynamic vocals, and relentless energy, their music is a cathartic release for fans of heavy music.

Their Best single Concrete set the tone for the band's direction: thick production, visceral lyricism, and a refusal to compromise. With a full alum release coming summer 2026, CHAOS ERA is just getting started.
