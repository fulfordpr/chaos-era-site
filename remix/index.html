Eligibility

  • Contest is open to participants worldwide
  • You must be 13 years or older to enter
  • Participants under 18 require parental/guardian consent
  • All submissions must be original work created by the entrant
  • One submission per person

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: WAV
  • Length: 2-6 minutes
  • Stems: Download official stems from the link provided
  • Creative Freedom: Any genre, any style - get creative!
  • Submission Method: Upload via the official submission portal
  • File Naming: YourName_TrackTitle_Remix.wav
  • Additional Requirement: Share a short, tiktok, or Instagram reel showcasing your remix process or final product and tag us on that respective platform

Judging Criteria

Submissions will be evaluated based on:

  • Creativity & Originality - 40%
  • Production Quality - 40%
  • Musicality & Arrangement - 20%

Important Dates

  • Contest Opens: 1/22/2026
  • Submission Deadline: 03/01/2026
  • Winners Announced: TBA

Terms & Conditions

  • By submitting, you grant CHAOS ERA exclusive rights to use, distribute, and promote your remix
  • Submissions cannot contain unauthorized samples or copyrighted material beyond the provided stems
  • CHAOS ERA reserves the right to disqualify entries that violate these rules
  • Winners will be contacted via email and must respond within 7 days
